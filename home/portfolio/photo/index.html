Chen Zhe's Photo Portfolio

To me, photography is more than just art. Because every time the shutter curtains click, my camera captures a moment that I can never relive again. Be it a moment of joy, surprise, tranquility or sorrow, photo is a window to the past, and it’s from the past that I can gain experience and strength to fight in the present.

Thunder

Taken at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Thunder is one of the hardest event to capture and I was lucky to get this shot. This is a single 15s exposure and the only postprocessings done were to dramatically recover the highlight (luckily I always shoot raw, albeit with drawbacks) and adjust the white balance.

  • Canon EOS 6D
  • EF24-70mm f/4L IS USM @ 44mm
  • S.S. 15s
  • A. f/8
  • ISO 100
  • Take at 2015/4/3 20:14

Fireworks, Singapore National Day Parade rehearsal

Fireworks are traditionally taken using the Black Card technique to stack more fireworks onto a single frame. However, this photo is a blended stack of 4 images in Photoshop. If you ever wonder why is the sky dark blue while the fireworks are going on, it’s because one of the stacked image is taken at the golden hour.

  • FUJIFILM X-T20
  • XF10-24mmF4 R OIS @ 10mm
  • S.S. 2.5s
  • A. f/13
  • ISO 200
  • Take at 2018/7/7 20:19
